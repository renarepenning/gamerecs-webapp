Instructions

Please create an account and use the 'Get Rec' tab to generate a recommendation. You may rate suggestions on the same page. All of your recs and ratings can be viewed under the User Home page. You must be logging in to use the app. Note that each email and password can only be used once per account.

Feel free to send any other questions or suggestions to renarepenning@gmail.com. The Mobile version is still in progress! Check out our timeline and product info under the 'About' tab.
